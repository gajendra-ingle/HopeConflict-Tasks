Information Technology

About Us

The department was established in 2001, to cater the needs of human resource requirements in the Information Technology section. Our slogan is: To enrich human resource with efficient and effective manpower in Information Technology. This department imparts the modern IT education to its students. An integrated teaching programme covering both theoretical and practical aspects of Information Technology made effective with Smart Class-room lectures/ training, demonstrations, illustrations, lab assignments with audio visual aids, projects, seminars, industrial tours and in-plant training. The inputs by the leading professionals in the field of IT services and management enable the students to keep abreast with current industry trends. Soft skills such as inter-personal communication are developed through workshop by experts.

Program Outcomes (POs)

- knowledge: Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.

- Problem analysis: Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.

- Individual and team work: Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.

- Conduct investigations of complex problems: Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions.

- Modern tool usage: Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.

Mechanical Engineering

About Us

The department was established in 1994. Now it is running with two sections of UG course and Post graduate course in M.E. CAD-CAM. Mechanical engineering is one of the basic engineering disciplines and it is integral part of all other industries and other branches of engineering. Mechanical engineering can see every walk of life and life without mechanical assistance is unimaginable which makes this branch stand out amongst all the field of engineering. This is an evergreen branch with immense opportunities for job and entrepreneurship. We at the Anuradha Engineering College constantly strive to impart industry practical oriented knowledge to the student in the field of designing, Manufacturing, Fluid power, Energy Conversion, Automobile, Heat Transfer etc.

Our students are placed in reputed organizations through campus recruitment like Indian Army, Mahindra and Mahindra, HSBC, TCS, VOLTAS etc. The departments has Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University recognized laboratory for carrying out Ph.D, research work and other research activities in the field of Mechanical Engineering. The department has Mechanical Engineering Student Association (MESA), involved in various students social activities like Seminars, Industrial visits , Guest Lecture, for professional awareness, Personality development, etc.

The Department has twelve well developed equipped laboratories namely Fluid Power. Heat Transfer, Engineering Metrology, Refrigeration and Air-conditioning, Measurement System, Energy Conversion, Automobile Engineering, Nonconventional Energy Sources, Theory of machine, Automation Engineering, CAD/CAM, Workshop, Machinshop.

The Machine shop is well equipped with computer Numerical Controlled lathe (CNC) trainer, Electro Discharge Machine, Kirloskar trainmaster, Center less grinder, Radial drill machine, Spark erosion, HMT Heavy duty lathe,etc. The department has well developed CAD laboratory having modeling, simulation, and legal licenses of analysis software like CATIA, ANSYS, MASTERCAM,etc.

The department has independent library with good collection of Books, national and international journals along with study material for competitive examinations like GATE,GRE,TOFEL,CAT,MAT,UPSC,MPSC,etc.

The department offers elective subjects such as IC Engines, Refrigeration and Air-conditioning, Production Planning and Control and Machine Tool Design. It is as per the Latest trends and requirement of mechanical engineering industries.

Chemical Engineering

About Us

The department was established in 1994, offers graduate, postgraduate (M.Tech), and doctoral (Ph.D) courses in chemical engineering. There is a great demand of chemical engineers in a wide spectrum of industries like petroleum refineries, basic chemicals, fertilizers, soaps and detergents, cements, pharmaceuticals etc. Of late, chemical engineers are making rapid strides and playing key roles in various emerging fields of science and technology, nuclear engineering, biomedical engineering, etc. The department provides basic knowledge of chemical engineering in addition to the technological understanding. The department also provides best supporting infrastructure for assimilation of new developments in the field. Latest equipments are made available in the laboratories to visualize the phenomenon under study and to Consolidate fundamentals of the subject. The department provide necessary professional training to the budding technocrafts by arranging lectures of the eminent personalities from the academic institutes of repute and industries. The department has well equipped laboratories namely chemical Engineering Operations, Industrial Waste Water Treatment, Membrane Separation Laboratory, Heat Transfer, Fluid Mechanics, Chemical Reaction Engineering, Process Dynamic and Control, Chemical Engineering and Analytical Laboratory.
Testing facilities like Chemical analysis using Gas Chromatography and Water Quality Analysis are available in the department. The students of the final year of Chemical Engineering work on various latest and relevant topics like production of bio-diesel, application of membrane separation technology, crystallization and simulation studies of distillation column etc. The students also prepare the working model to represent their projects. The department actively promotes industry-institute interaction by offering consultancy services to the industries in the surrounding areas. Alumni of the department are well placed in reputed organization / industries in India and abroad and some of them are pursuing higher educational abroad. Chemical Engineering Student Association (ChESA) provides platform for exchange of knowledge and information through interaction and organizes a National level Technical Paper Presentation every year.
